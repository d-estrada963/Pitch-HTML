Schedule

  • Week 1 (Oct 1 - Oct 7)
    • Develop a sketch (Axure RP prototyping) of what we want the app to look like and have a detailed plan of the app’s functionalities (with pictures)
  • Week 2 (Oct 8 - Oct 14)
    • Develop the home page that requires users to login or create an account.
    • Establish user account functionality and verification process.
  • Week 3 (Oct 15 - Oct 21)
    • Create interface for song review pages
      • Include genres, artists, albums
      • Data mine from database to gather sample music data for website
  • Week 4 (Oct 22 - Oct 28)
    • Establish the rating system and implement it to song pages
  • Week 5 (Oct 29 - Nov 4)
    • Add a review system and ability to display ratings outside of pages
  • Week 6 (Nov 5 - Nov 11)
    • Add “fun facts” tab and ability to add links to music streaming services
  • Week 7 (Nov 12 - Nov 18)
    • Give the user the ability to add songs to the database
      • Requires a streaming link that verifies the song
  • Week 8 (Nov 19 - Nov 25)
    • Add a playlist feature that can compile lists of songs and add ratings to them
  • Week 9 (Nov 26 - Dec 2)
    • Add “fun facts” and perfect the UI layout and program functionality before the final project presentation
  • Week 10 (Dec 3 - Dec 7)
    • Final Project Presentation
